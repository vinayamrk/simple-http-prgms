Web data services [1] refers to service-orientated architecture (SOA) applied to data sourced from the world wide web and the Internet as a whole. Web data services enable maximal mashup, reuse, and sharing of structured data (such as relational tables), semi-structured information (such as Extensible Markup Language (XML) documents), and unstructured information (such as RSS feeds, contents from web applications, commercial data from online business sources).
